Firmy

Firmy will help Russian speaking users locate Russian speaking businesses so that they will be able to communicate in a language that they both understand clearly.

Motivation: When ever I'm trying to find a business that can do the same job cheaper I sometimes turn to the Russian speaking community, but trying to locate the right business is a little hard because most of it is word of mouth.

Tech Stack: JS, React, Node, Mongoose, MongoDB, JWT, Passport, Bcrypt

Hike Carpool

Hike Carpool is a blog dedicated to helping hikers find other hikers that have room in their car's when they are going hiking or even share when they have room in their own cars.

Motivation: When ever I want to go hiking I usually have open seats in my car, and am always open to the idea of meeting new people on hikes.

Tech Stack: JavaScript, Node, MongoDB, Express, Passport, JWT, Mocha + Chai
